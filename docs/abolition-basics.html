Abolition not Regulation

For five decades, the response to the greatest moral atrocity of our age has been incremental regulation. We tracked the pulse, we measured the weeks, but we failed to establish Justice. Abolition is different.

Christian abolitionism is the application of God's law and the Gospel to the sin of child sacrifice, demanding equal protection for preborn image-bearers as for the born.

It means:

  • Equal protection under the 14th Amendment to the pre-born
  • Criminalizing abortion as homicide with no arbitrary age limits
  • No partiality based on location, age, or circumstances of conception
  • Defining life from fertilization—not heartbeat, not viability

Biblical foundation: Proverbs 24:11, Proverbs 31:8, James 2:1-9
