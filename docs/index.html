ABOLITION NOT REGULATION

For five decades, the response to the greatest moral atrocity of our age has been incremental regulation. We tracked the pulse, we measured the weeks, but we failed to establish Justice. This timeline chronicles the journey from the darkness of Roe to the rising light of the Equal Protection movement.

The Goal

To criminalize abortion as homicide, providing equal protection under the 14th Amendment to the pre-born.

The Hope

The Gospel of Jesus Christ, which commands us to rescue those being led away to death (Proverbs 24:11).

The Current Battleground

Georgia HB 441: Equal Protection

We have moved beyond the "Heartbeat Bills" that merely set a timing threshold for death. The Georgia Equal Protection Act (HB 441) represents the standard of justice required by God and the Constitution.

The Old Way: Regulation

  • Arbitrary Lines: Protects life only after a detectable heartbeat, 15 weeks, or viability.
  • Immunity: Often gives immunity to the mother or providers in certain cases.
  • Subservient: Often bows to federal court opinions (Roe) rather than challenging them.
The Standard

The New Way: HB 441

  • Total Abolition: Defines life from fertilization. No arbitrary time limits.
  • Equal Protection: Applies the exact same laws protecting born people to pre-born people.
  • Interposition: Asserts state sovereignty to protect life regardless of federal overreach.
