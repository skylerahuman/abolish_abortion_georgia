Why we must repent

You may hear prominent "Pro-Life" groups lobbying against HB 441. They argue that it is "too extreme," that it "hurts the movement," or that "women will be jailed."

This opposition stems from their commitment to the "Second Victim" narrative—the idea that a mother can never be guilty of abortion. Abolitionists believe that while mercy and grace are vital, the law must declare abortion to be murder, or else it is not truly prohibited.

HB 441 is the test: Do we really believe abortion is murder? Or is it just a bad choice that should be regulated?
