The Standard of Justice

HB 441 is not just another pro-life regulation. It is the application of God's Law to man's law.

The Core Doctrine

The Georgia Equal Protection Act (HB 441) seeks to do one simple thing: apply the existing laws against homicide to the unborn, without exception.

Under current Georgia law, if you kill a born person, it is murder. If you kill an unborn person, it is often treated as "healthcare" or a "choice," regulated by arbitrary timelines (heartbeat, 20 weeks, etc.).

HB 441 removes the loopholes. It asserts that from the moment of fertilization, a human being is a person made in the image of God, deserving of the exact same legal protection as you or I.

Man's Law (Regulation)

  • ✗ Arbitrary Lines: Protects life only after a "heartbeat" is detected.
  • ✗ Discrimination: Allows killing based on age (pre-heartbeat) or method.
  • ✗ Immunity: Often grants immunity to those who solicit or perform the killing.

God's Law (Equal Protection)

  • ✓ Total Protection: Protects life from the moment of fertilization.
  • ✓ Equal Justice: The penalty for killing a pre-born child is the same as killing a born child.
  • ✓ No Compromise: Rejects the idea that abortion is ever "healthcare."
